SECTION 1: VERBAL ABILITY

PART 4: SENTENCE STRUCTURE

PRACTICE TEST | 10-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 8

Directions: Choose the best sentence that follows correct grammar and proper sentence structure.

Answer Explanations:

  1. B – Apostrophe Misuse → "It’s" = "it is." Others misuse "its."
  2. A – Sentence Fragments → Complete sentence. B is a fragment.
  3. C – Degree of Comparison → "Smarter" is correct comparative.
  4. C – Subject-Verb Agreement → "The number" = singular → "is increasing."
  5. B – Dangling Modifier → Subject ("she") matches action.
  6. B – Redundancy → "Returned" already means "came back."
  7. B – Subject-Verb Agreement → "The group performs…" (singular).
  8. B – Word Usage → Correct is "He suggested that I join…"
  9. B – Active Voice → Clearer than passive versions.
  10. D – Redundancy / Wordiness → "The reason… is that…" is correct.
